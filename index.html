Bit more.

The estate is conveniently situated at 6 Kms from the Picturesque town of Mudigere.,about 22 Kilometres from the District Headquarters of Chikmaglur. The scenic Devaramane Hills, Charmudi Ghats, the unexplored Devegudda peaks are all within 20 Kms from the estate. The tallest Peak in Karnataka Mullayanagiri, Bababudangiri, Kudremukha, Kemmangundi,the temple town Dharmasthala and Horanadu, Rafting at Balur and Balehonnur are all about an hours drive from the estate. If you want to go out and visit the tourist attractions around Chikmalgur, you are at the right place. Transport arrangements will also be made on request.
